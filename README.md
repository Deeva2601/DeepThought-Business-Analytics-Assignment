# DeepThought Business Analytics Recruitment Assignment

This repository contains the completed recruitment assignment for the Business Analyst role at DeepThought. The assignment is divided into two parts: Target Company Research and a Scale-up Proposal.

## 📁 Repository Structure

- **[Part_A/](https://github.com/Deeva2601/DeepThought-Business-Analytics-Assignment/tree/main/Part_A)**
    - `federer_companies_ahmedabad_vadodara.csv`: Detailed profiling of 25 specialty manufacturers in the Ahmedabad-Vadodara hub.
    - `methodology.md`: Comprehensive explanation of the research strategy, hub selection, and data verification (C1-C6 scoring).
- **[Part_B/](https://github.com/Deeva2601/DeepThought-Business-Analytics-Assignment/tree/main/Part_B)**
    - `scale_up_proposal.md`: A scalable, automated proposal to identify and profile 1000 "Federer" companies across India using AI and human-in-the-loop verification.
- **`sourcing_funnel_sketch.jpg`**: A hand-drawn diagram illustrating the sourcing funnel, AI integration, and hallucination control logic.

## 🎯 The "Federer" Profile Logic
The research focuses on the **Federer** (Ideal Customer Profile) companies, defined by:
- **Location**: Mid-sized industrial hubs (Ahmedabad-Vadodara).
- **Revenue**: Rs. 50 Cr – Rs. 500 Cr.
- **Leadership**: Technocrat founders/MDs with a strong technical moat.
- **Sector**: Specialty Chemicals, Biotechnology, and Niche Manufacturing.

## 🔍 Scoring Criteria (C1-C6)
Each company was scored from 1-6 based on:
1. **C1**: Manufacturer Status
2. **C2**: India-based/Promoter-led
3. **C3**: Technical Moat (Differentiated products)
4. **C4**: Technical Decision Maker (Founder profile)
5. **C5**: Growing Sector (Tailwinds)
6. **C6**: Growth Signals (CAPEX, Capacity expansion)

## 🛡️ Hallucination Control
To ensure 100% data accuracy, a **Data Triangulation** methodology was used:
- Cross-referencing AI-generated insights with official ROC filings via Tofler/Tracxn.
- Verifying physical plant locations via GIDC/Industrial directory records.
- Human review of all "Federer" technical moats.
